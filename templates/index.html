VirtualJudgePY is not a real online judge. It can grab problems from other
regular online judges and simulate submissions to other online judges.
It aims to enable holding contests when you don't have the test data.

VirtualJudgePY currently supports the following online judges:

HDU,PKU,ZOJ,BZOJ,Codeforces,Aziu...... and so on
